# Story 54-5: Content backfill — `caverns_and_claudes/beneath_sunden` settlement rooms

## Business Context

Add typed `entities[]` blocks to the **settlement rooms** of `beneath_sunden` — the hand-authored above-ground / town YAML rooms under `worlds/caverns_sunden/rooms/` whose `description` is authored by hand (not generated by the cookbook). The procedural cavern rooms below ground get their manifests from the cookbook in Epic 55; this story is the *authored half* of the same world.

**Audience:** James and Sebastien (load-bearing playgroup) — beneath_sunden is the active campaign. Validator-clean settlement rooms are the demonstration substrate the live playgroup hits whenever the party returns to town.

**Expected outcome:** Every hand-authored room YAML in `caverns_sunden/rooms/` that carries an authored `description` also carries a typed `entities[]`. `pf validate locations caverns_and_claudes caverns_sunden` is clean on the settlement-room subset (procedural caverns are out of scope until 55-1 lands).

## Technical Guardrails

**Implementation plan:** `docs/superpowers/plans/2026-05-19-story-54-5-caverns-sunden-entities-backfill.md` — task-by-task authoring guide.

**Key files:**
- `sidequest-content/genre_packs/caverns_and_claudes/worlds/caverns_sunden/rooms/*.yaml` — find the settlement subset (`grep -l "^description:" sidequest-content/genre_packs/caverns_and_claudes/worlds/caverns_sunden/rooms/*.yaml` filters to authored rooms; cookbook-emitted rooms don't exist yet pre-55).
- `sidequest-content/genre_packs/caverns_and_claudes/worlds/caverns_sunden/npcs.yaml` — NPC binding source.
- `sidequest-content/genre_packs/caverns_and_claudes/pack.yaml` — `generic_allowlist[]` additions.

**Patterns to follow:**
- Same as 54-4: three-tier model, `tier=yes_and` reserved for runtime, legacy fields intact, no prose rewrites.
- The fixture seeded in 54-2 (`sunden_square.yaml` with two entities) is the *minimum*; this story expands it to the full authored entity set per the spec §4.2 "Procedural settlement" row.

**What NOT to touch:**
- Procedural cavern rooms (no authored `description` — they're generated by Epic 55).
- The materializer / cookbook (Epic 55 owns those).
- 54-2's fixture entry — extend it; do not delete.

## Scope Boundaries

**In scope:**
- Typed `entities[]` on every hand-authored settlement room in `caverns_sunden/rooms/`.
- NPC + location_feature bindings.
- Per-pack `generic_allowlist[]` additions specific to caverns_and_claudes prose.
- Validator pass on the authored subset.

**Out of scope:**
- Cookbook-emitted cavern rooms (Epic 55).
- Other worlds in caverns_and_claudes (if any exist).
- New scenario content.

## AC Context

**AC-1:** Every hand-authored settlement room YAML in `caverns_sunden/rooms/` carries a non-empty `entities[]` block.

**AC-2:** Bindings to NPCs and location features resolve under 54-3's validator.

**AC-3:** `pf validate locations caverns_and_claudes caverns_sunden` reports zero hard errors over the authored room set.

**AC-4:** No entity carries `tier=yes_and`.

**AC-5:** The 54-2 seeded fixture (`sunden_square.yaml`) is expanded, not deleted. Existing tests against it continue to pass.

**AC-6:** Manual smoke: launch `just up`, advance to sunden_square / any authored settlement room — prose unchanged; the 54-9 Location panel (once it lands) shows the typed manifest server-side.
