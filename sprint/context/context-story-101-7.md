## Problem
daemon.py is a 1,290-LOC god module hosting the socket server, heartbeat writer, CLI parsing, EmbedWorker, RenderService, warmup/shutdown/status, AND dispatch — and dispatch is half-extracted: dispatch_request() (daemon.py:168) handles ONLY tier=music while image tiers are routed by a parallel inline if/elif chain in _handle_client (methods at lines 429-897); the function's own docstring admits image renders 'never reach this function'. Two dispatch mechanisms for one method is the embryo of the next split-brain. Scope: (1) unify render dispatch — all tiers route through one dispatcher with loud ValueError on unknown tier (No Silent Fallbacks); (2) extract EmbedWorker and RenderService into their own modules (media/embed_worker.py, media/render_service.py or similar), leaving daemon.py as socket lifecycle + routing only; (3) preserve the heartbeat and warmup contracts exactly — server's DaemonStateMirror (story 45-31) and daemon_client are consumers; do NOT change the wire protocol. Pairs with 101-5 (flux alias retirement) — sequence after it to avoid churn. Wiring test: existing socket integration tests must pass unchanged, plus one test proving every advertised method reaches the unified dispatcher.
