## Problem
Latent bug in apply_witnessed_act (sidequest-server/sidequest/game/political_engine.py, merged Plan 2): the final threshold pass fires collapse for any premise at/under its collapse threshold and tip for any bloc at/over its tipping threshold EVERY call, with no guard that the dial actually moved THIS turn. A world authoring a bloc at/above its tipping_threshold (or a premise at/under collapse) would tip/collapse on the FIRST unrelated witnessed act, even one that didn't touch that bloc/premise. Add a touched-this-turn guard so collapse/tip only fires for dials mutated by the current act (or that crossed the threshold as a result of this act's drain/couple/awaken), not for dials already past threshold at rest. Include a regression test: an unrelated act with a bloc pre-seeded at/above threshold must NOT tip it. Discovered during Plan 2; deferred out of Plan 2b (router-only).
