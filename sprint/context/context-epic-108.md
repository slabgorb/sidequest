## Overview

Both are removed, not tuned. Invariants preserved: ADR-116 (Other required; single room-sourced seater — this subsumes the 107-2 MM-seater finding), ADR-139 (win-condition liveness, HP durability, mechanically-capable Other via authored opponent_damage), ADR-114 (ablative HP). OTEL: the lie detector must prove native is OFF (wn.native_scaffolding_suppressed). Scope: WN COMBAT only (hp_depletion); dial chase/negotiation confrontations keep the native dial engine even in WN packs and are untouched. Rollout WWN-first (beneath_sunden in play; heavy_metal/elemental_harmony/barsoom swept the same way); swn/cwn/awn same doctrine, staged. NO rebalancing of any WN number — that's the whole point. Refs: ADR-143, ADR-117 (RulesetModule), ADR-142 (WN core extraction, doc-gap), ADR-033, ADR-116, ADR-139, ADR-114; wn_round.py, beat_kinds.py, dispatch/dice.py (seam ~671-676), game/ruleset/without_number.py.
