## Acceptance Criteria

### AC1: Command Points shared resource
- CP is a per-vessel shared resource in the War Rig crew confrontation's shared state
- Three CP actions implemented per SWN §4.3:
  - **Do Your Duty** (0 CP cost, standard resolution)
  - **Above and Beyond** (1 CP cost, +1d6 to the roll or pool)
  - **Support Department** (1 CP cost, assist another station's action)
- CP pool persists across rounds and depletes as actions are taken
