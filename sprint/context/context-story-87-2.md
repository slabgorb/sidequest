## Problem
heavy_metal was bound to `ruleset: wwn` with ablative-HP combat in Story 1 (87-1, merged),
but its **classes and chargen are still 5e**: `rules.yaml` carries a D&D-5e `allowed_classes` /

both the genre and the live `evropi` world `char_creation.yaml` crucible scenes hand out
`class_hint`s like Fighter/Warlock/Cleric/Wizard/Monk. There is no `classes.yaml`. A player
creating a heavy_metal character today picks 5e classes against a WWN engine.
