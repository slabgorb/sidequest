## Problem
Render a new 'History' section on the character sheet, starting with the origin block. New collapsible/labelled 'History' section in CharacterSheet.tsx that reads the creation_answers payload from 93-2 and lists, per scene, the prompt and the player's answer (their verbatim words for freeform, their chosen label for selections). Scenes marked archetype_inferred get a small badge ('inferred from your words') so the player sees where the engine read their freeform. The section is structured as the future home for player-linked lore (93-4 attaches lore there) — so build the container/heading to host more than the origin block, but render ONLY the origin block now (no lore stubs). Add the creation_answers type to payloads.ts. Audience: makes chargen provenance legible in the player UI (Sebastien/Jade) and gives narrative-first players (James) their own words back. Refs: CharacterSheet.tsx ~101-242; payloads.ts CharacterSheetData ~22-88; depends on 93-2 snapshot field.
