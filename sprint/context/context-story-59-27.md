## Problem
Playtest 2026-06-02 (wry_whimsy/oz solo, SM/GM). The IntentRouter cutover is LIVE and DONE (59-1 social-confrontations-never-engage, 59-4 atomic cutover, 59-10 feed-genre-confrontation-types-to-router-prompt all done), yet across a full session the ONLY confrontation type that ever fired was `escape` (movement) — and only after extreme player escalation. The verbal/social types NEVER fired even on direct, committed prompts. This is a FRESH defect in the SHIPPED router, not a duplicate of the done cutover work.

EVIDENCE:
- Turn 5 (world-initiated menace): walk into the poppy field, soft "push through before it takes hold." Resolved entirely in prose; `game_patch.extracted ... beat_selections=0 confrontation=None`. The world's purpose-built Wonder-Shock type (intent_verbs: [endure, marvel, resist, anchor, withstand]) did NOT fire.
- Turn 6 (explicit committed resist, "I will NOT go under… fight for every step"): an `escape` confrontation finally instantiated (Poppy Field seated as Other, ADR-116 working) — but only after heavy player commitment, and as movement not Wonder-Shock.
- Turn 10 (committed verbal demand at the Emerald City gates — textbook Persuasion / Audience-Trial: "I'm not moving until I get a straight answer… convince me he's worth the walk"): `game_patch.extracted ... beat_selections=0 confrontation=None`. Narrator handled it as a lovely prose evasion — ZERO mechanics.

So the trigger is biased toward physical/movement escalation and misses verbal/social confrontations, which is most of what a whimsy/social genre is made of (6 authored types: Audience/Trial, Wit-Duel, Escape, Wonder-Shock, Persuasion, Violence).

LIKELY ROOT CAUSE (same neighborhood as the 59-1 tea_and_murder finding): the per-pack DERIVED intent_verb_set for wry_whimsy's verbal confrontation types is too narrow to match natural prose, so the live router's Haiku pass doesn't dispatch confrontation:{persuasion,audience_trial,wit_duel,wonder_shock} on verbal intent. Confirm against the router prompt (intent_router_pass.py execute_intent_router_pre_narrator_pass) + wry_whimsy/rules.yaml derived verb sets. Also weigh the design question: should a menace auto-enter a confrontation (turn 5) or only on player commitment (turn 6)? Either is defensible — pick one and encode it.

NOTE: the INVISIBILITY half of the poppy-field finding (no beat/dice/dial UI) was a SEPARATE server bug already fixed (PR slabgorb/sidequest-server#575, classless-pack confrontation surfacing) and is awaiting verify — this story is ONLY the TRIGGER sensitivity. ADRs: 113 (Intent Router), 123 (Mechanical-Engagement Pipeline).
