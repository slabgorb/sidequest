## Dev Decisions

- **Music is cinematic, not video game.** Film soundtrack behavior — overtures, cues, one-shots. Not looping BGM. a2a variations from canonical themes, not random text2music.
- **Dinkus and drop caps are CSS-based.** No image assets needed. Don't generate or audit them.
- **ACE-Step for music generation.** Must use ACE-Step venv: `~/Projects/ACE-Step/.venv/bin/python`. Music is pre-rendered files in genre_packs, not daemon-generated.
- **Conlang system is a core feature.** Corpus/Markov conlang belongs in world-builder skill, not a footnote.
- **World building creative split.** Keith owns mechanics/crunch. World Builder gets creative freedom on flavor/lore/story. Keith wants to be surprised.
